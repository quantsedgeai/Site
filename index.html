Frequently Asked Questions

Your most common questions, answered.

Is this platform for beginners? add
While our platform is powerful enough for seasoned quants, it's designed with an intuitive interface. If you have a solid understanding of trading concepts, you'll be able to leverage our tools. We also provide extensive documentation and tutorials to get you started.
How do you ensure my funds are safe? add
Your security is our highest priority. QuantsEdge is non-custodial via Turnkey, meaning we never have access to your funds. You connect your wallet (e.g., MetaMask, Ledger) and all trades are executed via our heavily audited smart contracts directly from your wallet.
What kind of AI do you use? add
Our core is a proprietary suite of machine learning models, primarily focusing on reinforcement learning and predictive analytics. These models analyze vast datasets of market activity, liquidity, and on-chain data to identify patterns and adapt strategies in real-time for optimal performance.
Which blockchains and DEXs are supported? add
We support all major EVM-compatible chains, including Ethereum, Arbitrum, Base, Avalanche, and BNB Chain. Our smart router aggregates liquidity from dozens of DEXs like Uniswap, Sushiswap, Curve, and more to ensure best-price execution.
What is the pricing model? add
QuantsEdge operates on a performance-based fee model.
