Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-05

This Privacy Policy explains how QuantsEdge ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you access our website and Services.

1. Information we collect

  • Contact information you provide (e.g., name, email) when requesting access or contacting us.
  • Usage data such as pages visited, device/browser info, and approximate location derived from IP.
  • Log data and diagnostics to maintain security and performance.
  • Integration metadata (if applicable) limited to non-secret identifiers to support non-custodial execution. We do not collect your private keys.

2. Cookies and similar technologies

We may use cookies or similar technologies to provide core functionality, remember preferences, and measure aggregate usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

3. How we use information

  • Operate, secure, and improve the Services.
  • Respond to inquiries and provide support.
  • Analyze aggregate usage to enhance reliability and user experience.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce Terms.

4. Sharing

We may share information with trusted service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics, email) under contracts requiring appropriate safeguards. We do not sell your personal information.

5. Data retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the Services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements, after which we delete or anonymize it.

6. Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect information. No system is perfectly secure; you are responsible for protecting your credentials, API keys, and wallets.

7. International transfers

Your information may be processed in jurisdictions with different data protection laws. We use appropriate safeguards where required.

8. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (e.g., GDPR/CCPA), you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal information. To exercise rights, contact us at privacy@quantsedge.ai. We may need to verify your identity.

9. Children

Our Services are not directed to children under 16. If you believe a child provided personal information, contact us to request deletion.

10. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted here with an updated date.

11. Contact

Questions about this policy: privacy@quantsedge.ai.
