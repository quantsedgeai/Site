Cookie Policy

Last updated: September 21, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how QuantsEdge Inc. ("QuantsEdge", "we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit quantsedge.ai and any linked pages (collectively, the "Site").

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device that gather and remember information about how you use a website. They help us run core site features, analyze performance, and tailor the experience to your preferences.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Strictly necessary cookies: Required for the Site to operate, including page navigation, security, and form submissions. These cookies cannot be switched off in our systems.
  • Performance cookies: Collect aggregated analytics about how visitors use the Site. This data lets us improve load times, page flows, and the content you interact with.
  • Functionality cookies: Remember choices you make, such as language settings or saved form details, to deliver a consistent experience between sessions.
  • Third party cookies: Some analytics and embedded services we rely on set their own cookies to measure usage or deliver content. These providers are listed in the section below.

Third Party Services

The Site may use third party analytics or infrastructure tools that place cookies in your browser. These providers are bound by their own privacy policies and use data to supply aggregated metrics or deliver platform services to QuantsEdge. We currently evaluate any new tool for security, data minimization, and regional compliance before enabling it on the Site.

How to Manage Cookies

You can control cookie settings through your browser. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies, or to receive an alert before they are stored. The following links explain how to adjust settings in popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Apple Safari
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Microsoft Edge

If you disable cookies, some parts of the Site may not function properly or may lose certain personalized features.

Do Not Track

Some browsers include a Do Not Track ("DNT") signal. Because a consistent industry standard has not been adopted, QuantsEdge does not currently respond to DNT signals. We will update this policy if that changes.

Updates to This Policy

We may modify this Cookie Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will revise the "Last updated" date above and, if the changes are material, provide additional notice through the Site or via email where appropriate.
