Frequently Asked Questions

Your most common questions, answered.

Is this platform for beginners? add
While our platform is powerful enough for seasoned quants, it's designed with an intuitive interface. If you have a solid understanding of trading concepts, you'll be able to leverage our tools.
How do you ensure my funds are safe? add
Your security is our highest priority. QuantsEdge is non-custodial via Turnkey, meaning we never have access to your funds. All trades are executed via audited smart contracts directly from your wallet.
Which blockchains are supported? add
We support all major EVM-compatible chains, including Ethereum, Arbitrum, Base, Avalanche, and BNB Chain. Our smart router aggregates liquidity from dozens of DEXs for best-price execution.
